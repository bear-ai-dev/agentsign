## What this Privacy Policy covers

This Privacy Policy covers how we treat Personal Data that we gather when you access or use our Services. "Personal Data" means any information that identifies or relates to a particular individual and also includes information referred to as "personally identifiable information," "personal information," or "sensitive personal information" under applicable data privacy laws, rules, or regulations. This Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of companies we do not own or control or people we do not manage.

## Personal Data we collect

We may collect the categories of Personal Data below from contributors.

### Profile and contact data

Includes your first and last name, email address, phone number, languages spoken, and any skill or expertise tags you provide. We collect this to provide, customize, and improve the Services; operate the contributor marketplace and process payouts; correspond with you; and for fraud prevention, security, and debugging.

### Account credentials

Includes your Acme User ID and authentication tokens. We do not store your password in plaintext. We collect this to provide access to your account and secure the Services.

### Payout data

Includes financial account information, payment method, last 4 digits of any card on file, billing address, and tax-related information. We collect this to process your payouts, comply with tax reporting requirements, and prevent fraud.

### Government-issued ID and identity-verification data

Where we collect government-issued ID or identity-verification data for KYC or anti-fraud purposes, we use it to verify your identity and eligibility to receive payouts and to comply with anti-money-laundering and tax laws.

### Submission metadata

Includes task ID, validation results, file format, file size, duration, language tag, and timestamps associated with your Submissions. The contents of Submissions themselves are governed by our Data Use Policy, not this Privacy Policy.

### Communications data

Includes messages you send to support, survey responses, and your communication preferences. We collect this to respond to you and to improve the Services.

### Device and IP data

Includes IP address, device ID, operating system, browser type, and crash logs. We collect this to provide, customize, and improve the Services; operate the contributor marketplace; correspond with you; and for fraud prevention, security, and debugging.

### Web analytics and approximate geolocation data

Includes page interactions, referring webpage or source, non-identifiable request IDs, IP-address-based location, and similar approximate location signals. We collect this to provide, customize, and improve the Services; market the Services; localize the Services; prevent fraud; and comply with sanctions and export-control laws.
